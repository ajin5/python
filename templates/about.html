About Chinninambi

CNE started in 2019 to distribute known brands of products existing in the market. As the current trend has shifted to natural Ayurveda and organic products, the company ventured to personal care and cure products. All the products are outsourced from a reputed house of manufacturing units. They are scientifically researched in reputed labs. Safety and efficacy are clinically tested. The company works on identifying the active molecules that enhance the properties of the ingredients of products, so as to achieve the optimum benefits. Hence the formulations are unique and efficient.
